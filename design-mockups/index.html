Three directions for the redesign.

The current product is functional but neutral. Football has a strong visual culture — broadcast graphics, kit design, magazine spreads, sticker albums — and the platform should borrow from it instead of looking like a generic dashboard. Below are three internally-coherent directions. Open each, scroll the whole page, then pick the mood that fits.
